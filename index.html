Privacy Policy for PRDog

Last updated: 2025-03-29

Introduction

Welcome to PRDog ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Content Information

  • We temporarily access the text content of your current browser tab only when you actively click the "Optimize" button
  • This content is used solely for real-time analysis and is not stored on our servers
  • The content is immediately discarded after analysis is complete

Technical Information

  • Browser language settings for providing appropriate language interface
  • Basic extension settings and configurations
  • API communication logs for service functionality

How We Use Your Information

Content Analysis

  • To provide real-time PRD optimization suggestions
  • To improve the quality of your product requirement documents
  • To enhance the analysis accuracy of our service

Service Improvement

  • To debug and maintain the extension
  • To improve user experience
  • To develop new features

Data Protection

Security Measures

  • All communications are encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS
  • No user content is stored on our servers
  • API keys and configurations are stored locally in your browser

Third-Party Services

  • We use DeepSeek API for content analysis
  • Only necessary content is transmitted to DeepSeek servers
  • No personal identification information is shared with third parties

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what information is being processed
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of any future updates
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Data Retention

  • Document content is processed in real-time and immediately discarded
  • Local settings are stored in your browser and can be cleared at any time
  • No long-term data storage on our servers

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on our Chrome Web Store page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Policy
